Module Epsilon

Resilience Rituals

High-trust cultures survive crises better than infrastructure alone.

Infrastructure is fragile; community is anti-fragile. We schedule these rituals to build the "Social Mycelium" that holds the node together.

Weekly • Every Sunday

The Skill Share

One hour, one neighbor, one skill. From "How to wire a solar panel" to "Fermentation 101." Knowledge transfer prevents dependency.

Monthly • Full Moon

The Council Circle

Governance without bureaucracy. A face-to-face check-in for the 150-person node to review resource ledgers and resolve conflict before it festers.

Seasonal • Solstices

The Repair Festival

We empty our closets of broken things and fix them together. Mending clothes, soldering electronics, and reinforcing the idea that nothing is truly waste.

Autarky Win: Reduced consumption, increased social capital, and the elimination of "learned helplessness."
