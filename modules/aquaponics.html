Module Zeta

Aquaponics & Water Sovereignty

The protein-engine of the urban node.

We decouple from industrial agriculture by bringing the ecosystem indoors. Aquaponics mimics natural waterways to produce protein (fish) and carbohydrates (plants) in a single loop.

Operational Specs

-90%Water Use
3xGrowth Rate
ZeroFertilizer

The Cycle

Fish Tank (Tilapia/Catfish)

Ammonia Waste Production

⬇ Bio-Filter (Nitrifying Bacteria)

Converts Ammonia → Nitrates

⬇ Grow Beds (Leafy Greens)

Plants absorb Nitrates (Cleaning Water)

⬇ Clean Water Returns to Tank
Autarky Win: A 200 sq ft system can provide 60% of the protein needs for a 4-person family year-round, independent of supply chains.

Water Capture

This system is fed by Rainwater Harvesting (Module Theta). Roof catchment systems filter directly into the aquaponic top-off tanks, ensuring the node is water-neutral in temperate climates.
