REIMAGINING URBAN EXISTENCE

The Future is Grown, Not Built.

Not a daycare, but a metaphorical Urban Paradise. We are redesigning cultural and social behavior to foster cooperation, local networks, and true autarky.

Sociology

Social Architecture

Applying Dunbar's Number to urban planning. Communities are capped at 150 active peers to ensure accountability and deep social bonding without the alienation of mega-cities.

Action: Decentralized "Village Nodes" within high-density blocks.
Psychology

Cognitive Urbanism

Leveraging Biophilic Design to reduce cortisol levels. Fractals in architecture and pervasive greenery are not just aesthetic‚Äîthey are cognitive health interventions.

Action: 40% Green Coverage Mandate for all vertical surfaces.
PolSci & Economy

The Gift Economy 2.0

Moving beyond transactionism. Liquid Democracy tools allocate shared resources in real-time, fostering a culture where giving enhances social capital more than hoarding.

Action: "Library of Things" & Open-Source Fabrication Labs in every district.
Cybernetics

Radical Autarky

Closed-loop systems for energy and food. Vertical farming handles 80% of caloric needs locally. Waste is nutrient input.

Action: Rooftop Aquaponics & Solar Glass integration.
Anthropology

Ritual & Play

Reclaiming public space for "Infinite Games". Life is not a race to production, but a collaborative canvas for expression and collective joy.

Action: Car-free "Play Zones" replacing central arteries.
